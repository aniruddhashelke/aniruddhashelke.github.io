T20 Cricket world cup analysis

Cricket is a sport I have been watching since my childhood and is very close to my heart, so I decided to make a project based on the T20 cricket world cup. Utilizing Pandas, I processed, cleaned, and transformed extracted T20 Cricket World Cup 2022 data from ESPNcricinfo, converting the tournament statistics from JSON to a more structured CSV format. With the data prepared and ready for analysis, I set out to create an interactive Power BI dashboard highlighting the standout players of the 2022 ICC Mens T20 World Cup.

By selecting relevant tournament metrics analyzing individual player statistics, I aimed to discover the top 11 performers of the competition based on a holistic analysis of the available data. The resulting dashboard visualizes these key performance indicators in an intuitive layout, focusing on the highest run scorers, wicket takers, and impactful all rounders that made their mark on the championship matches. This data preparation and analysis exercise provided me with invaluable experience using Python and Power BI to gain insights from raw data.
