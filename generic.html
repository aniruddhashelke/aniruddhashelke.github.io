Background

Aniruddha is an aspiring data engineer and analyst currently pursuing his MS in Information Systems at the University of Maryland's Robert H. Smith School of Business, expected to graduate in December 2024. He holds a Bachelor of Engineering degree from the University of Mumbai in India, where he gained proficiency in languages like Python, R, and SQL as well as data analysis tools including Pandas, NumPy, and Matplotlib. During his internship at Xangars Labs, he gained valuable experience in SQL query optimization, database performance improvement, and supporting business decisions through data analysis.

Through internships and academic projects, Aniruddha has hands-on experience building data pipelines, transforming data, designing databases, performing statistical analysis, and creating interactive dashboards and visualizations. He has worked with real-world datasets across domains such as crime statistics, YouTube data, transportation data, and sports analytics. His projects demonstrate skills in collecting, wrangling, analyzing large datasets and synthesizing insights.

Aniruddha has spearheaded workshops on Python and led student groups, illustrating his teaching abilities and leadership potential. He has been recognized for excelling in project management simulations and database design competitions. As his academic and professional experiences highlight, Aniruddha has a passion for data-driven decision making and translating raw data into impactful visualizations.

What I am currently working on

Aniruddha is continuously engaged in upskilling himself in data engineering and cloud technologies. He is currently reading "Fundamentals of Data Engineering" by Joe Reis and Matt Houseley to strengthen his knowledge of constructing data pipelines, data modeling, distributed data storage, and data governance. To complement this, he is actively learning Apache Spark and Hadoop to master big data processing. Additionally, Aniruddha is preparing for the Azure Fundamentals certification to gain a well-rounded understanding of Microsoft Azure cloud services. Through these focused efforts on training and certification, Aniruddha aims to excel as a data professional equipped to apply his skills in cloud contexts. His self-motivated learning trajectory displays his dedication to staying updated with the latest advancements in the data landscape.
